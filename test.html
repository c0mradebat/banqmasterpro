About Rajasthan Royal

Creating Unforgettable Experiences

Founded in 2005, Rajasthan Royal Banquet Hall has been the preferred destination for celebrating life's most precious moments. Our heritage-inspired venues blend traditional Indian elegance with modern comfort to create the perfect backdrop for your special occasions.

Whether it's a grand wedding, corporate event, or cultural celebration, our team of experts ensures every detail is meticulously planned and executed to perfection. We take pride in our authentic Indian hospitality and attention to cultural nuances that make each event truly memorable.

Our state-of-the-art facilities, coupled with our commitment to excellence, have made us the choice of hundreds of couples and event planners across the region.
